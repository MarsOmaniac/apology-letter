My Apology

Dear Malu,

With a heart heavy with regret, I write to you today. I have come to realize that I have made an unforgivable mistake, and in doing so, I have caused a rift in our bond, a bond that meant more to me than words can describe.

I cannot sleep. Every moment that passes, I am consumed by the thought of what I have done. My actions were thoughtless, and I am burdened by the distance I have created between us. I can only wish for the chance to make amends, to return to the friendship that once brought me joy.

But I know that such things are not so easily repaired. Perhaps I do not deserve the forgiveness I so desperately seek, but I cannot help but hope that, in time, you may find it in your heart to forgive me.

Until then, I remain your friend, sincerely and truly regretful.

- Mahi

Thank you...

With heartfelt sincerity, Mahi
